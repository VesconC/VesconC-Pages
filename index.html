Introduction

VESCON CONSTRUCTIONS LIMITED is a PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY founded on 23-Sept 2020 in Nairobi, Kenya. This company was started with a vision to provide the best possible experience and construction services to the clients. The management team of the company sports a diverse portfolio with immense work experience and thorough knowledge of the construction practices in Kenya. At VesconC Ltd., we strive to deliver projects with excellent quality factoring in budget and time. We have a combined experience of working in general construction, civil construction, and roadworks.

One of our directors, Pravin Chowhan is also the director of the company: Shiv Krupa Enterprises limited, and has been undertaking construction work in and around Nairobi since 2016. The list of works taken is enumerated on the projects page.
